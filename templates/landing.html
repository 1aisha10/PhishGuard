Background
About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is a multi-layered URL threat analysis tool built to go beyond simple blocklist lookups. It combines a custom-trained machine learning model with real-time signals from five external APIs to deliver a confident verdict on any URL in under five seconds.

The ML model was trained on 70,000+ labeled URLs and extracts structural features like URL length, digit ratio, special character density, entropy, and TLD risk score. Google Safe Browsing and VirusTotal provide threat-intelligence cross-validation, while HuggingFace AI adds a deep-learning second opinion.

DNS/WHOIS lookups surface hosting anomalies and newly registered domains — a strong indicator of phishing infrastructure. SSL certificate inspection, brand impersonation detection, and live screenshots round out the analysis so you have the full picture before clicking anything.

The built-in AI chat (powered by Groq) lets you ask plain-language questions about any scan result — no security expertise required.
